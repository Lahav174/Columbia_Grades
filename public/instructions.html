How to find your A-Ranges

Note: If you already have your transcript or know how to get a copy, skip to step 6

  1. Visit SSOL. Log in.

  2. Under the "Transcripts"/"Transcript Ordering" section, there will be a button that says "Order Transcript" at the bottom of the page. Click it.

  3. This will redirect you to a new website. You may have to log in with your uni. After logging in, select eTranscript.

  4. Follow through with the following prompts like a typical online purchase. Assuming you don't choose any extra features (like tracking) this won't cost you a dime, although you will be asked to provide payment information for some reason.

  5. After completing your order, you should recieve two emails in your inbox within the next 2-10 minutes. The latter should contain your transcript.

  6. In your transcript, there lists every class you have taken each semester thus far. In the right most collumn, you should see the A-Range for each class you have taken. Students in SEAS do not have access to this information.
